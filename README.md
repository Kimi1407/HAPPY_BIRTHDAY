# 🎂 Happy Birthday - Interactive Sliding Puzzle

An interactive birthday greeting featuring a **3x3 sliding puzzle game** with confetti celebration! Solve the puzzle by rearranging the photo pieces to reveal the complete birthday image.

## ✨ Features

- **Interactive Sliding Puzzle** - 3x3 grid puzzle that requires strategic thinking
- **Drag & Drop Support** - Move pieces using mouse drag and drop
- **Touch Screen Compatible** - Fully functional on mobile devices with touch controls
- **Confetti Celebration** - Automatic confetti burst when puzzle is solved
- **Birthday Themed Design** - Festive background, banner, and styling
- **Responsive Layout** - Adapts perfectly to desktop, tablet, and mobile screens

## 🎮 How to Play

1. Click the **"CLICK TO CONTINUE"** button on the landing page
2. The puzzle pieces are scrambled randomly
3. **Swap adjacent tiles** (up, down, left, or right) to arrange the picture
4. Arrange all pieces in the correct order: 1, 2, 3, 4, 5, 6, 7, 8, 9
5. **Celebrate with confetti** when you complete the puzzle! 🎉

### Controls:
- **Desktop:** Drag and drop tiles to swap them
- **Mobile:** Tap and swipe to move tiles
